Questions

Everything you need to know.

What is Enchantra?
Enchantra is a tarot reflection app that helps you build a deeper relationship with your own tarot deck. Draw cards from your physical deck, explore their meaning, record your reflections and discover patterns across your readings over time.
What is a reflective tarot reading?
A reflective tarot reading helps you explore your cards rather than predict your future. You draw from your own physical tarot deck, and Enchantra interprets the card in the context of your intention, your tarot tradition and the lunar cycle. It offers a place to begin. The meaning is always yours to make.
Do I need to know tarot already?
No. Enchantra is designed for beginners and experienced tarot readers alike. If you're new to tarot, it explains each card in plain English while encouraging you to build confidence in your own intuition over time.
Can I use my own tarot deck?
Yes. Enchantra is built to work alongside your own physical tarot deck. It supports major tarot traditions, including Rider-Waite-Smith, Thoth and Marseille. You choose your deck's tradition, and each card is interpreted within its own system so the meaning stays true to the creator's intention.
Does Enchantra predict the future?
No. Enchantra is not a fortune telling app. It helps you reflect on your readings, record what they mean to you and notice patterns across your practice over time. Rather than telling you what will happen, it helps you pay closer attention to the life you're already living.
How does AI work in Enchantra?
AI helps explain the symbolism and traditional meanings of your cards while remembering your readings, reflections and patterns over time. It doesn't replace your intuition or tell you what your reading means. Instead, it gives you a thoughtful place to begin. Every meaning remains yours to make.
Does AI make up my interpretation?
No. Enchantra uses AI to provide thoughtful interpretations based on your chosen tarot tradition, your intention and your reading history. It never tells you what to believe. Your own reflections and lived experience are always the most important part of every reading.
Is my tarot journal private?
Yes. Your readings, reflections and notes are private by default. They're securely stored so you can revisit them whenever you like, but they are never shared with other users.
Can Enchantra help me notice patterns?
Yes. As your readings grow, Enchantra helps you discover recurring cards, repeating themes and, if you choose, real world echoes like dreams, symbols, people, places and meaningful moments. These are the connections that often only become visible with time.
Is Enchantra a digital tarot deck?
No. You continue using the physical tarot deck you already own and love. You shuffle, draw and read your own cards. Enchantra simply helps you understand, remember and build a deeper relationship with them over time.
How is Enchantra different from other tarot apps?
Most tarot apps focus on digital card draws or one-off readings. Enchantra is designed to help you build an ongoing tarot practice. It combines your physical deck, thoughtful interpretations, private journalling and long-term pattern tracking, so every reading becomes part of a bigger story.
When will Enchantra be available?
Enchantra is currently in development and available by waitlist. Joining is free, and waitlist members will receive early access on iPhone and Android as soon as it's ready.
